Vazon Beach

Guernsey's most popular west coast beach. Perfect for surfing, swimming, and watching the sunset. Facilities, parking, and cafe nearby.

Best surfing beach in GuernseyLifeguard patrol in summerBeach cafe & toiletsDisabled access

Vazon has been a popular recreation spot since Victorian times when Guernsey became a tourist destination.

Things to Do

Surfing

Swimming

Sunbathing

Beach volleyball

Rock pooling

Facilities

  • Public toilets
  • Beach cafe
  • Car park (paid)
  • Disabled access
  • Lifeguard (summer only)

Visitor Tips

Tip: Check surf forecast - best waves with westerly winds
Tip: Arrive early on summer weekends - parking fills up
Tip: Cafe closes at 5pm in winter
Tip: Watch the sunset - best view on the island
